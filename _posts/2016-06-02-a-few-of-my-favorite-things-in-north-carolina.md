**Christmas in Wilmington**<figure id="attachment_4557" style="width: 437px" class="wp-caption alignnone">

<img class="size-full wp-image-4557" src="http://exploringducky.files.wordpress.com/2016/05/christmas-in-wilmington1.jpg" alt="Christmas in Wilmington, NC" width="437" height="510" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Christmas in Wilmington is delightful. For extra fun, the big kids at heart can turn this into a bit of a pub crawl (providing someone is willing to be designated driver) stopping at various bars along the way for appetizers and drinks.</figcaption></figure>
